## ‚ö†Ô∏è KEY RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Payment Infrastructure
**Challenge**: Limited digital payment adoption in Zimbabwe
**Solution**:
- Multi-currency support (USD, ZWL, ZAR)
- Mobile money integration (EcoCash, OneMoney)
- Agent-based payments (pay at retail stores)
- International payment options (PayPal, Stripe for diaspora)

### Risk 2: Low Purchasing Power
**Challenge**: Students have limited disposable income
**Solution**:
- Micro-pricing ($1-5 price points)
- Group discounts (university packages)
- Scholarship to pay for premium (earn credits)
- Ad-supported free tier (always available)

### Risk 3: Competition
**Challenge**: WhatsApp groups, Facebook already used
**Solution**:
- Unique value: Scholarship database (#1 feature)
- Better discovery than WhatsApp
- Structured communities vs. Facebook chaos
- Mobile-first, data-friendly design

### Risk 4: Internet Costs
**Challenge**: Data is expensive in Zimbabwe
**Solution**:
- Partner with telcos for zero-rating
- Lite/offline modes
- Image compression
- Optional low-data mode
